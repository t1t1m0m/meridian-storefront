The craft

Materials chosen for the next ten years, not the next season.

01

Block acetate

Every acetate frame starts as a solid sheet from Mazzucchelli in Italy — milled, tumbled and hand-polished over days, never injection-moulded.

02

Beta-titanium

Our wire frames use aerospace beta-titanium: it flexes to your face and springs back true, at a third of the weight of steel. Nickel-free throughout.

03

Lenses that earn it

From G-15 to polarized and our cobalt mirror, every lens is anti-reflective coated and cut to spec. Optical frames ship Rx-ready for your optician.
